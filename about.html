WHO WE ARE

There are many Information of CONTAI INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOLOGY

CONTAI INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOLOGY (CIT) is the fastest growing and the best college in Purba Midnapore offering undergraduate courses in Hospitality Management (BHM), and Bachelor in Computer Application (BCA) and BBA in Hospital Management. The Institute was established in the year 2013 by Hon’ble Chairman – Mr.Nripen Kanti Rana with a vision to establish a premier educational hub and create an environment where value-based quality education can prevail in the society and students can become professionally skillful and ready for the Industry. The institute is affiliated to the Maulana Abul Kalam Azad University of Technology (formerly known as WBUT, recognised by UGC), with College Code – 340. Since its inception, the institute always focused on imparting value-based quality education. CIT has developed unique learning initiatives for the all-round development and result oriented academic performance of the students. These initiatives are the outcome of the experienced faculty members who understand the needs and demands of the present Industry.
